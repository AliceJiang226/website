Home Alone

"Home Alone" is a beloved holiday comedy about 8-year-old Kevin McCallister, who is accidentally left behind when his family goes to Paris for Christmas. Kevin must defend his home from two bumbling burglars using clever traps and his quick wit, all while discovering the true meaning of family and the holiday spirit.
